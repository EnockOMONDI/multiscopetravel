Client’s questions

FAQ: Everything You Need to Know

What services does Multiscope offer for corporate clients?

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including air ticketing, accommodation booking, visa assistance, conference management, car hire, airport transfers, and customized travel itineraries.

Can Multiscope Travel handle last-minute travel arrangements?

Yes, we specialize in managing last-minute travel needs. Our team is available 24/7/365 to assist with urgent bookings and changes to ensure a seamless experience.

How do you ensure cost-effective travel solutions for businesses?

We leverage our strong partnerships with airlines, hotels, and other service providers to secure competitive rates. Additionally, we work closely with clients to optimize travel policies and manage budgets efficiently.

Can Multiscope Travel assist with international travel requirements, such as visas and work permits?

Absolutely. We provide full support for visa applications and work permits, guiding you through the process and ensuring all necessary documentation is in place.
